Europa Clipper Mission

Last year, NASA launched a mission to take a closer look at one of Jupiter's moons, Europa. Scientists believe that the ocean-covered moon could be the best place for hosting life in our solar system (other than Earth of course). The spacecraft, Clipper, will do dozens of close, high speed passes of the moon, taking up close pictures and other interesting measurements. Because it is so far away, we wont reach the moon until 2030, but it is something to look forward to!
